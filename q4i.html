q4i

q4i is a research team focusing on theoretical aspects of Quantum for Information Processing and Computation. q4i can be thought of as abbreviations of “Quantum for Information” or “Quantum for Interest”. q4i team consists of Siddhartha Das as the Principal Investigator and the following members.
